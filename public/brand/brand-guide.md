# LogiOnboard AI Brand Guide

## Colors
- **Navy Blue**: #1E3A5F
- **Amber**: #F59E0B
- **White**: #FFFFFF

## Typography
- **Font**: Inter
- **Weights**: Bold (700) for headers, Regular (400) for body.

## Assets
- **favicon.svg**: Shield + Check + Truck icon.
- **logo-horizontal.svg**: Main logotype.
- **logo-icon.svg**: Standalone mark.
- **badge.svg**: Marketing seal.

## Usage
- Use the horizontal logo for headers.
- The badge is for footers and marketing materials.
- Maintain adequate padding around the logo.
